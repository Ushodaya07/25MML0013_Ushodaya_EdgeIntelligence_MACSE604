## Overview

This repository demonstrates a **basic audio data digitalization pipeline** using a real-world audio dataset.  
The objective of this lab is **not model training**, but to understand:

- How audio data is collected from a dataset
- How audio is converted from sound into numerical form (digitalization)
- How numerical audio samples are represented as vectors
- How dense feature vectors are extracted from digital audio
- How these vectors are organized for Edge Intelligence applications

This work is intended as a **first laboratory experiment** in the subject **Edge Intelligence**.

---

## Dataset Used

### ESC-50: Environmental Sound Classification Dataset

- Contains **2,000 audio clips**
- Each clip is **5 seconds long**
- Covers **50 environmental sound classes**  
  (e.g., dog barking, rain, car horn, engine, footsteps, etc.)
- Audio format: **WAV**
- Widely used in academic research and labs
